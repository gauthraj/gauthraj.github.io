OpenBadges

Show your skills to the World

Open Badges are new online standard to recognize and verify learning. Open Badges are excellent ways to show your skills and achievements in the form of digital badges. Badges can be earned from multiple resources, including Organizations and Individuals.

We help schools, colleges and other related institutions with the implementation of Open Badges in their learning and teaching process. Adapting to Digital Badges, will help students to show their skills and achievements in various platforms like Websites, Blogs, Social Networks, etc that can bring them more opportunities.

Digital Literacy Programs

Build skills to master the technology

Our Digital Literacy Programs aims to help you understand Nuts & Bolds of technology. Digital literacy is the knowledge, skills, and behaviors used in a broad range of digital devices such as smartphones, tablets, laptops and desktop PCs. A Digitally Literate person will be able find, capture and evaluate the information from various digital sources and posses critical thinking.

We offer different levels of Digital Literacy programs including the much needed Web Literacy to people of different age and background. We continuously develop and test our own curriculum for all our Literacy Programs based on these principles, 1.Connected Learning, 2.Interest-Driven Learning, 3.Learning by Making, 4.Inquiry-Driven Learning and 5.Participatory Learning.

We aim to empower learners with the digital skills that best suits them for skill development, Personal growth, career opportunities and finally to their fullest potential.

"We are different! We just teach the better ways to learn anything!"

Moodle

Make Learning Anytime! Anywhere!

Not everyone able to catch up everything taught at school/university. This may be due to different reasons. But, People learn better when it's of their own interest, own speed and at convenient place. Recent growth in e-learning platform proves this as fact. Wouldn't you be interested in offering your students an online learning platform that is easy and simple to manage by yourself ? With Moodle, you can do!

Moodle is a single, robust and online learning management system that can be filled with dynamic courses, extending learning anytime and anywhere. Moodle also enables teachers to facilitate student discovery, creativity and opportunity.

We help with the implementation of moodle in schools, colleges and other relevant organizations. Moodle websites can be hosted in organizations server and made accessible only to a private group. It can also be hosted on our servers and made accessible anywhere - students can access and learn even from their home.We also provide training in managing, Customizing and using moodle at different levels. You can either pick from our huge-variety of courses available or create your own courses in few simple steps. You can easily customize the learning experience with gradable assignments, quizzes and even collaborative activities.

"We make your Online Learning Scalable, Effective, Fun and Easily manageable"
